By providing us with this Personal Information, you are consenting to our use of it in accordance with this Privacy Notice.

B. Non-Personal Information.
This Application may collect the following non-personal information: information about what country and language (e.g., English, French, Spanish, etc.) you selected to use for viewing videos through the Application. In addition, we collect the following information from the Application: whether the user views, shares, or deletes the SAWBO video or Application, as well as a time and date stamp for any of those activities. By using the Application, you are consenting to our collection, storage, and use of such information in accordance with this Privacy Notice.

User Personal Information is collected and used to customize the Application for your use, to provide the University with statistics on usage of the Application, for University's research, educational use, public service, and/or for improving services provided through the Application. Before geo-location information is deleted, it will be anonymized by generalizing to the particular city/region and country. Non-personal information, including anonymized collective location information from multiple users may be used for any purpose, including in academic journal articles to demonstrate use and impact of this Application.
